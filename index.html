Bipedal Humanoid Robot Platform

Inverted Pendulum Dynamic Control System

February 2026 – Present

C++ state-space control system with eigenvalue analysis to stabilize a highly dynamic physical plant in real time. Built a sensor fusion pipeline fusing high-speed rotary encoder data into high-frequency PID loops. Full hardware integration of stepper motors with systematic signal noise debugging.

C++ State-Space Control Sensor Fusion PID Stepper Motors Eigenvalue Analysis
Finalizing integration
03

6-DOF Robotic Arm with Cycloidal Actuators

November 2025 – January 2026

End-to-end 6-axis robotic arm designed in SolidWorks with custom 3D-printed cycloidal gearboxes to eliminate backlash. Full ROS 2 control architecture with tf2 coordinate frame transforms, URDF/launch files, and forward/inverse kinematics. Closed-loop PID tuned on all six axes.

ROS 2 SolidWorks tf2 PID Control Inverse Kinematics Linux
04

Robotic Manipulator with Planetary Gearing

September – November 2025

Custom planetary gearing system designed in SolidWorks and integrated into a full robotic arm assembly. Programmed closed-loop brushless motor control in C++ with a modular architecture covering motion planning, feedback control, and calibration routines.

SolidWorks C++ Brushless Motors Motion Planning Closed-Loop Control

6-DOF Servo Robotic Arm Prototype

The origin of the series. Initial robotic arm concept designed in SolidWorks with component selection and procurement. ROS control architecture with motion planning for trajectory execution — the foundation for four increasingly complex iterations.
